My Style

I am mostly inspired by Google's material design. My style is simple, minimal, and clean. I think that design in pop culture is all abou flashy and loud trends, but I think otherwise. I think design should be simple and clean with an ease of navigation and ability to enjoy what you see.

My Origin

By utilizing elements and principles of Material Design, we were able to create a framework that incorporates components and animations that provide more feedback to users. Additionally, a single underlying responsive system across all platforms allow for a more unified user experience.

I am a very simple card. I am good at containing small bits of information. I am convenient because I require little markup to use effectively. I am similar to what is called a panel in other frameworks.
